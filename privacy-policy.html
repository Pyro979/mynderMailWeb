Privacy Policy for mynderMail

Introduction

mynderMail ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the mynderMail mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Email addresses (recipient email, quick action configurations)
  • Message content (stored locally on your device)
  • App preferences and settings

Information We Don't Collect:

  • We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers
  • We do NOT track your usage or behavior
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking services (except crash reporting - see below)
  • We do NOT share your data with third parties (except crash reporting service - see below)

Crash Reporting (Optional):

  • Crash reporting is optional and can be enabled/disabled in Settings
  • If enabled, we use Sentry to collect crash reports and error logs
  • Sentry may collect: device information, app version, error messages, stack traces, device identifiers
  • Crash reports help us fix bugs and improve the app
  • Sentry's Privacy Policy: https://sentry.io/privacy/
  • You can toggle crash reporting on or off in Settings at any time
  • Crash reporting is disabled by default

Google Account Information:

  • When you sign in with Google, we use OAuth to authenticate
  • We only request permission to send emails (Gmail API send scope)
  • We do NOT read your emails, access your contacts, or modify your account settings
  • Google account information is stored securely on your device using Android's secure storage

How We Use Your Information

Local Storage:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • Messages are stored in a local database (SQLite/Room)
  • Preferences are stored in local device storage
  • Drafts are saved locally using SharedPreferences

Email Sending:

  • Messages are sent through Google's Gmail API
  • Emails are sent from your Google account to your specified recipient
  • Email content is transmitted securely through Google's servers
  • We do NOT have access to the content of emails sent

Data Security:

  • All local data is encrypted using Android's security features
  • OAuth tokens are stored securely using EncryptedSharedPreferences
  • No data is transmitted to our servers

Third-Party Services

Google Gmail API:

  • We use Google's Gmail API to send emails
  • Your use of Gmail API is subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • We only request the minimum necessary permissions (send emails only)
  • Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Sentry (Crash Reporting):

  • We use Sentry for crash reporting and error tracking (if enabled)
  • Sentry collects crash reports, error logs, and device information to help us fix bugs
  • Data collected may include: device model, OS version, app version, error messages, stack traces
  • Sentry does NOT collect your email content, messages, or personal information
  • Sentry's Privacy Policy: https://sentry.io/privacy/
  • Sentry's Data Processing Addendum: https://sentry.io/legal/dpa/

Data Retention

Local Data:

  • Messages are retained according to your configured history retention setting
  • You can configure retention limits in Settings (0, 1, 10, 100, or 1000 messages)
  • Unsent messages (queued/failed) are always retained until sent or deleted
  • You can delete messages manually at any time
  • Drafts are cleared when messages are successfully sent

Account Data:

  • OAuth tokens are stored on your device
  • You can sign out at any time, which removes stored tokens
  • Signing out does not delete your messages or preferences

Your Rights

Access:

  • You can view all your messages in the History screen
  • You can export or copy message content

Deletion:

  • You can delete individual messages
  • You can clear all messages
  • You can reset all app data (Factory Reset in Admin/Debug mode)
  • Uninstalling the app removes all local data

Control:

  • You control all app preferences
  • You can disable features (notifications, etc.) at any time
  • You can revoke Google account access in your Google account settings

Children's Privacy

mynderMail is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: app@uxiomatic.com

Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Other applicable privacy laws
